Welcome to my UX Portfolio! I'm Alison Mazzarella-Wölzl, a UX designer dedicated to crafting captivating user experiences through technology. With a Bachelor's in Applied Technology, a Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM), and a Google UX Design certificate, my journey in UX spans an internship at an independent software company and successful freelance consulting for small businesses.

Passionate about enhancing man-computer symbiosis and advocating for improved app accessibility, especially for underrepresented groups, I'm committed to utilizing design to bridge the gap between users and technology. My goal is to create inclusive, user-centric solutions that make a meaningful impact.

Currently, I'm enthusiastically seeking opportunities to contribute my skills and passion to real-world projects. I'm eager to expand my expertise through challenging roles that drive innovation and create substantial value.
