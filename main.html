About Me

Willkommen! I'm Alison Mazzarella-Wölzl, a passionate UX designer committed to crafting engaging user experiences through technology. I hold a Bachelors in Applied Technology, am a Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM), and possess a Google UX Design certificate. My journey in UX includes an UX design internship at an independent software company and two freelance UX consulting projects for small businesses.

My fervor lies in man-computer symbiosis and advocating for increased app accessibility, particularly for underrepresented groups. I'm driven to leverage design to bridge the gap between users and technology, striving to create inclusive and user-centric solutions.

Currently seeking opportunities to contribute my skills and passion to real-world projects, I aim to further expand my expertise through internships that challenge me to create value and foster innovation.
