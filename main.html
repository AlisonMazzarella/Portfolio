Welcome to my UX Portfolio! I'm Alison Mazzarella-Wölzl, a passionate UX designer dedicated to shaping exceptional user experiences at the intersection of technology and human interaction. Armed with a Bachelor's in Applied Technology, a Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM), and a Google UX Design certificate, I've cultivated my UX skills through hands-on experiences, including an enriching internship at an independent software company and successful freelance consulting for small businesses.

What captivates me most about UX is the opportunity to enhance man-computer symbiosis and champion improved app accessibility, particularly for underrepresented groups. I believe in the power of design to bridge the gap between users and technology, making it more inclusive and impactful. My focus is on creating solutions that not only meet user needs but also contribute positively to the broader community.

As I envision my future in UX, I am committed to staying at the forefront of innovation. I am particularly excited about contributing to projects that push boundaries and challenge the status quo. My goal is to leverage my skills to create meaningful, user-centric solutions that address real-world problems. I am actively seeking opportunities that will not only expand my expertise but also allow me to make a substantial impact on the field of UX.
